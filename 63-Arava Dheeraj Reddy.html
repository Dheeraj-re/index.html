About Us

A Legacy of Learning Since 1999

Greenwood Academy was founded with a single purpose: to create an inspiring learning environment where every student can discover their strengths, build critical thinking skills, and grow into confident individuals.

We combine a rigorous academic curriculum with a broad range of co-curricular activities — arts, sports, technology, and community service — to develop well-rounded students ready for the challenges of the modern world.

  • ✓ CBSE-affiliated with state-of-the-art infrastructure
  • ✓ Smart classrooms and a fully-equipped science lab
  • ✓ Dedicated counsellors and mental-health support
  • ✓ Annual sports meet, cultural fest, and hackathons

Our Courses & Programmes

From foundational sciences to cutting-edge technology, our curriculum is designed to nurture curiosity and build expertise.

🔬

Grade 9–12

Science & Technology

Hands-on experiments in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology backed by modern lab facilities. Prepares students for competitive engineering and medical entrance examinations.

⏱ 3-year programme 👩‍🏫 18 teachers
📐

Grade 6–12

Mathematics & Logic

From arithmetic foundations to advanced calculus and statistics — our maths programme builds analytical thinking, problem-solving, and a love for numbers.

⏱ 7-year programme 👩‍🏫 12 teachers
💻

Grade 8–12

Computer Science

Scratch and block coding for beginners, Python and C for intermediates, all the way to web development, databases, and AI basics for senior students.

⏱ 5-year programme 👩‍🏫 10 teachers
📊

Grade 11–12

Commerce & Economics

Accountancy, Business Studies, and Economics — taught with real-world case studies and industry visits. Ideal foundation for CA, MBA, and Finance careers.

⏱ 2-year programme 👩‍🏫 8 teachers

Grade 1–12

Arts & Humanities

Language arts, History, Geography, Civics, and Fine Arts. Develops communication, creativity, and cultural awareness — skills every professional needs.

⏱ 12-year programme 👩‍🏫 22 teachers

All Grades

Sports & Physical Education
